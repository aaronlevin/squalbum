how to play

Your goal is to guess the album title! Click on squares to reveal portions of the album cover beneath. Some notes:

  • guess only the album title and not the artist.
  • ignore the "difficulty" slider (I mean, feel free to play with it but its under development and can be used to cheat)
  • if you want to play again, click clear state and hit refresh
  • comming soon: you will be able to create your own dubble and share with friends.
